Overview

This project is an establishment of AghaJee Builders offering apartments of 3- and 4-Bedrooms, in different sizes. It is a residential project with its main offerings being apartments flats , however, for the comfort of its residents, we also offer shops for sale. Both the property types; apartments and flats consist of a Kitchen, Attached Bathrooms, Bedroom(s), Drawing Room and a TV Lounge. The kitchen is designed in an American style, meting out sufficient space for you to cook and keep your cooking ware and crockery in. The TV lounge will be spacious enough to let you dine, watch TV and have a good chit chat with the family. The tubewell will provide fresh water to the residents, whereas, other techniques will also be used to supply water, 24/7. For vertical transportation, there will be state of the art elevators that will take you to the desired floor without any hassles – smoothly and speedily. There will be a dedicated basement floor for car parking, providing you with sufficient space. A separate maintenance team will be responsible for the cleanliness, management and operations. Our project is carefully designed to be well ventilated with fresh air coming in. While all the facilities are available within a kilometres reach, the safety and security of the residents is not compromised upon at all. There will be a 24/7 security system taking care of both, the apartments. The security system will include well-trained personnel who will be looking after the people and monitor everything through CCTV cameras.
